üìã Degree Program Prerequisites & Requirements

Minimum Academic Requirements
  • Overall KCSE Grade: Minimum C+ (8 points) across all subjects
  • Cluster Points: Varies by university and program (typically 20-80 points)
  • Core Subjects: English and Mathematics required for most programs
  • Subject Combinations: Science or Humanities depending on course choice
  • Age Requirement: Usually 18+ years old at time of entry
Program-Specific Requirements
  • Engineering Programs: Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics all B- or better
  • Medical Programs: Biology, Chemistry, Physics (min C+)
  • Humanities Programs: Strong English and subject-specific papers required
  • Business Programs: Mathematics and any other subject
  • Law Programs: English and Social Studies/History recommended

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about KUCCPS and university admission

What are cluster points?

Cluster points are KUCCPS's scoring system based on your KCSE grades in specific subjects. They determine which university programs you can apply for. Learn more about cluster points

What's the minimum grade for university?

The minimum mean grade is C+ for university degree programs. However, competitive courses may require higher grades. Check admission requirements

When is the application deadline?

The KUCCPS application deadline varies yearly, typically in March/April. Check the official KUCCPS website for exact 2026 dates.

Can I change my course choices?

Yes, KUCCPS allows multiple revision windows where you can change your course choices. Revision typically happens in April and June.

What if I don't qualify for degrees?

You can pursue diploma or certificate programs as alternatives. These programs lead to employment and offer pathways to upgrade to degrees. Explore diploma options

Are there scholarships available?

Yes! HELB loans, government scholarships, private scholarships, and institutional aid are available. Discover funding options

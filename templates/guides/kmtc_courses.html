About Kenya Medical Training College (KMTC)

Kenya Medical Training College (KMTC) is the leading government institution for training health professionals in Kenya. With multiple campuses across the country, KMTC offers recognized diploma programs in various healthcare fields. KMTC graduates are highly sought after by the Ministry of Health, private hospitals, and international healthcare organizations.

Why Choose KMTC?

  • Government Institution: Quality-assured training with international standards
  • Recognized Qualifications: KMTC diploma is highly valued across East Africa
  • Multiple Campuses: Training centers in Nairobi, Kisumu, Nakuru, Mombasa and more
  • Career Security: High demand for healthcare professionals in Kenya and globally
  • Further Studies: Pathway to degree programs at universities
  • Competitive Salary: Good earning potential and career progression

KMTC Campuses in Kenya

KMTC Training Locations:

  • Main Campus - Nairobi: Capital city location with diverse programs
  • Kisumu Campus: Serving Western Kenya region
  • Nakuru Campus: Serving Rift Valley region
  • Mombasa Campus: Coastal region training center
  • Kisii Campus: Highlands region programs
  • Nyeri Campus: Additional Eastern region location

Choose based on proximity and specific program availability at different campuses.

Employment & Career Prospects

KMTC graduates enjoy excellent employment prospects with multiple career pathways:

  • Ministry of Health Placement: Direct recruitment into government hospitals and health centers
  • Private Healthcare: High demand in private hospitals and clinics
  • International Opportunities: Many countries recruit Kenyan-trained healthcare professionals
  • NGO Positions: Health-focused NGOs value KMTC qualifications
  • Self-Employment: Opportunities to start private clinics or health businesses
  • Degree Pathways: Upgrade to degree programs at universities for higher roles

Balancing Study & Work

  • Full-time programs are intensive requiring dedicated study time

Frequently Asked Questions About KMTC

Which KMTC campus should I choose?

KMTC has campuses across Kenya. Choose based on proximity to your home/region and specific program availability. Check the KMTC website for program offerings at each campus.

What are the job prospects for KMTC graduates?

Excellent! Healthcare professionals are in high demand in Kenya and globally. Most KMTC graduates are employed by the Ministry of Health or private healthcare institutions.

Can I study part-time at KMTC?

Some programs allow part-time schedules for working professionals. However, clinical and practical components require consistent, full-time presence during internship periods.

Can I upgrade from KMTC diploma to a degree?

Absolutely! KMTC graduates can pursue degree programs in health sciences at various universities. Many universities have special pathways for KMTC diploma holders.

What's the starting salary for KMTC graduates?

KMTC graduates employed by the Ministry of Health earn competitive salaries with benefits. Specific amounts depend on the program and position level. Private sector salaries are often higher.

How to Check KUCCPS Courses 2026

Complete step-by-step guide to finding and checking all available university and college programs

Last Updated: January 2026 Read time: 8 min Category: Application Guide

Why Checking KUCCPS Courses Matters

The Kenya Universities and Colleges Central Placement Service (KUCCPS) is the official body responsible for placing KCSE graduates into universities and colleges. Knowing how to check available courses helps you make informed decisions about your educational future. This comprehensive guide walks you through the entire process.

1

Access the Official KUCCPS Portal

Visit the official KUCCPS student portal at students.kuccps.net. You'll need your KCSE index number and year of examination to log in.

Pro Tip

Bookmark the portal for easy access throughout the application period. Save your login credentials securely.

2

Calculate Your Cluster Points

KUCCPS uses cluster points to determine course eligibility. These are calculated from your KCSE grades in specific subject combinations required for each course. Understanding your cluster points is essential.

4

Review Course Requirements

For each course you're interested in, check:

  • Minimum Grade Requirement: What mean grade is required?
  • Cluster Points Needed: How many cluster points must you have?
  • Subject Requirements: Which specific subjects are required?
  • Institution Details: Where is the course offered?
  • Program Duration: Is it 4, 5, 6 years or a diploma/certificate?

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I forgot my KUCCPS password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the KUCCPS portal. You'll need your KCSE index number and examination year. Password reset instructions will be sent to your registered email address.

Can I check courses before officially applying?

Yes! Our KUCCPS Courses Checker lets you explore available courses anytime using your KCSE grades. The official KUCCPS portal opens during application windows.

What's the minimum grade for degree courses?

Minimum is C+ (mean grade) for most university degree courses. However, highly competitive courses like Medicine may require A- or A grades. Check specific course requirements.

Can I change my course choices after submission?

Yes, during official revision periods. KUCCPS typically allows 2-3 revision windows where you can modify your course choices. Check the KUCCPS website for exact revision dates.

What if I'm not qualified for any degree courses?

You can pursue diploma or certificate programs as alternative pathways. These programs also lead to employment and give you the option to upgrade to degrees later.

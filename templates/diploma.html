Diploma Program Prerequisites and Requirements

Minimum Academic Requirements
  • Overall KCSE Grade: Minimum C (5 points) or equivalent
  • Cluster Points: Generally lower than degrees (10-40 points)
  • Core Subjects: English required; Mathematics required for most technical courses
  • Entry Requirements: Form 4 completion with reasonable grades
  • Age Requirement: Usually 17+ years old at time of entry
Program-Specific Requirements
  • Engineering Diplomas: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics required
  • Nursing/Health Diplomas: Biology and Chemistry requirements
  • IT/Computing Diplomas: Mathematics and Computer Studies helpful
  • Business Diplomas: English and Mathematics; accounting subjects beneficial
  • Hospitality Diplomas: No strict subject requirements; practical aptitude important

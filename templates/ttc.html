Teacher Training Program Prerequisites and Requirements

Minimum Academic Requirements
  • Overall KCSE Grade: Minimum C (5 points) - higher for secondary streams
  • English Language: Minimum C for communication skills essential
  • For Secondary: Minimum C in teaching subject required
  • Cluster Points: Typically 40-60 points depending on TTC
  • Age Requirement: Usually 18+ years at entry
Program-Specific Requirements
  • Primary Teaching: Love for working with children, communication skills
  • Secondary Teaching: Strong subject knowledge in chosen specialization
  • Technical Teaching: Practical aptitude and technical subject knowledge
  • Special Needs Education: Compassion, patience, and commitment to inclusion
  • All Programs: Good character, health screening, and teaching aptitude
